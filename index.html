About us

Sc Hook srl was founded in 1994 by capt. Vasilica Neculai and since then maintains high standards of in-depth knowledge and proven expertise in the logistics industry.

Sc Hook srl acts on behalf of importers, exporters or other companies or persons, organizing the safe, efficient and cost-effective transportation of goods.

We offer the best transportation solutions for all your logistic needs at highly competitive prices.

The knowledge and expertise of our personnel and the extensive carrier network of Hook will guarantee the safe, secure and speedy delivery of your cargo.


PRESS RELEASE 02.08.2021 - PROJECT START NOTICE

HOOK S.R.L. announces the launch of the project entitled “GRANT APPLICATION FOR WORKING CAPITAL REQUESTED BY HOOK S.R.L” project no. RUE 11711 / 22.10.2020 registered within the Measure “Grants for working capital”, established by GEO no. 130/2020.

The project runs for a maximum period of 12 months, starting with the date of signing the financing contract with the Ministry of Economy, Energy and Business Environment / AIMMAIPE, respectively 02.08.2021.

The objective of the project is to support HOOK S.R.L. by providing financial support from non-reimbursable external funds related to the Competitiveness Operational Program 2014-2020, in the context of the crisis caused by COVID-19 and Order approving no. 1060/2857/2020 of the state aid scheme - Support for SMEs in the view of overcoming the economic crisis generated by the COVID-19 pandemic.

The main results of the project include the following:
  1. - maintaining the activity for a period of at least 6 months,
  2. - maintaining the number of jobs compared to the date of submission of the application, for a period of at least 6 months, on the date of granting the grants


The value of the project is 157,311.90 RON (total value) of which: 136,792.95 RON grant and 20,518.95 RON co-financing.

Project co-financed by the European Regional Development Fund through the Operational Program Competitiveness 2014-2020
